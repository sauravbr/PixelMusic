Crafted for Audiophiles

Pixel Music isn't just another player. It's a passion project built entirely open-source, prioritizing your experience and privacy above all. No ads, no tracking—just pure, unadulterated music streaming pulling directly from the vast library you already love, wrapped in an interface that responds to your every touch.

Lossless Audio Support

Experience your favorite tracks in the highest fidelity available, with advanced equalizer settings to tune the sound to your ears.

Dynamic Theming

The interface adapts seamlessly to the album art of the currently playing track, providing a deeply immersive visual experience.

Zero Telemetry

Fully open-source and fiercely private. Your listening habits remain yours alone. No accounts required, no data harvested.
